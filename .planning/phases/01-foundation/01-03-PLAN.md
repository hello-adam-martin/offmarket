<objective>
Fix DM Sans italic font loading to close FOUN-07 verification gap.

Purpose: The DM_Sans call in fonts.ts omits the `style` parameter, so italic DM Sans is never loaded from Google Fonts. Two existing pages use Tailwind's `italic` class (wanted/[id]/page.tsx, claim/[code]/page.tsx). Adding `style: ['normal', 'italic']` loads both variants properly.

Output: Updated fonts.ts with italic DM Sans loaded from Google Fonts.
